# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and technically rigorous collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and professional.
- Focus on facts, reproducible evidence, and clear tradeoffs.
- Assume good intent and avoid personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, personal insults, or intimidation.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of test or security results.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
Actions may include warning, temporary restrictions, or removal from collaboration channels.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the repository maintainer.
All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
